Our Practice Areas

Practicing in property rights litigation and property law in North Louisiana.

Expropriation/Appropriation

Louisiana’s constitution allows government “takings” of private property, but that doesn’t mean property owners have to take it lying down. Touchstone Law Firm is focused on this area, guiding clients through legal options and rights.

Partitions and Quiet Title

We practice in handling partition actions and quieting title, helping our clients to properly divide real estate and solve title issues. Our understanding of these matters enables us to provide legal assistance tailored to your situation.

Successions and Boundary Disputes

We offer legal guidance in matters of successions and boundary disputes. Whether it’s a dispute over property lines or the legal process of inheritance, we focus on providing support to meet our clients' unique needs.

Foreclosures, Timber Piracy, and More

From foreclosures to timber piracy, contracts of sale, and legal access issues, our practice encompasses various aspects of property law. We are committed to understanding each client's circumstances and offering appropriate legal guidance.
