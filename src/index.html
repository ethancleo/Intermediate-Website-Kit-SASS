About Us

Founded by David M. Touchstone, Touchstone Law Firm has been serving the people of North Louisiana for over three decades. David's deep respect for Louisiana’s civilian legal tradition and history informs our unique approach to legal practice.


With a focus on collaboration, David has served on committees of the Louisiana Law Institute, working with some of the state’s greatest legal minds to draft property rights legislation. Our firm's expertise extends across various property-related matters, including expropriation, foreclosures, boundary disputes, and more.


At Touchstone Law Firm, we believe in personalized and client-centric legal support. Whether it’s a complex real estate dispute or personalized legal guidance for timber piracy, we strive to ensure optimal legal protection for our clients. Our commitment to property law has enabled us to create a legacy of excellence and integrity in Shreveport & Bossier City.
